The Game

Control the titular hero through a fantastical handcrafted world. Explore a variety of different landscapes as you jump, swing, pull and tear your way to victory. Do you have what it takes to rescue your friend and uncover the mystery in BOXMAN!

Boxman is a third-person puzzle platformer remeniscent of titles such as Little Big Planet and Tearaway. Coming soon to PC, Xbox, Playstation and Switch. Add to your Steam wishlist today and stay tuned for our demo.
